Host tips

  • Grab each photo straight from the linked source and crop out captions/watermarks so nobody can reverse-image-search mid-round.
  • Front-load the easy babies, then build to the sneaky old-timers (7–10) for a strong finish.
  • The classic trap: an animal that looks young but is ancient (Elvis, Fatou). Award a bonus point for anyone who resists the obvious "middle" answer.
  • Ages drift over time — re-verify at the source before a future game night, especially the babies.
